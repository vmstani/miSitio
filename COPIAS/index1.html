About me

Who I am:

A passionate Designer

I am located in Buenos Aires, Argentina. I graduated as a Multimedia Designer in 2015 at ISEC and since then I have continued my training in User Experience Design. I am passionate about designing, making models and prototypes. Designing is for me finding solutions that improve a person's quality of life, either through a product or a service. I consider myself a creative and detail-oriented person. I enjoy every stage of the design process, from researching, empathizing with users and proposing ideas. I am very curious. I like to learn new tools that help me in my work.
